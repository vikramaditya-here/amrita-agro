Our Story

Rooted in the Soil of Haryana

Born in the heartland of Haryana, Amrita Agro Industries began with a simple belief — that what comes from the earth, in its purest form, is what nourishes best. Our production unit in Shekhupur Droli, Fatehabad carries the spirit of generations of farming families.

We blend time-honored agricultural knowledge with modern manufacturing practices to create products that meet the real needs of livestock farmers and rural communities. Every batch we produce is a commitment to purity and the trust our retailers have placed in us.

We believe in strengthening the rural economy — by creating local employment, supporting farmers, and supplying reliable, high-quality agro products to distributors and retailers across the region.

Mustard Oil

Mustard Oil

Cold-pressed from the finest mustard seeds of Haryana. Our mustard oil preserves the authentic aroma, pungency, and traditional taste that Indian kitchens have trusted for generations.

  • Cold-pressed for maximum purity
  • Rich natural aroma & bold flavor
  • Free from additives & preservatives
  • Sourced from local mustard farms

Churi / Mixture

A carefully balanced blend of agro by-products crafted to provide supplementary nutrition to cattle. A trusted, affordable option for farmers looking for reliable daily feed.

  • Improves digestion & energy levels
