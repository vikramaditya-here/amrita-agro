Our Story

Rooted in the Soil of Haryana

Born in the heartland of Haryana, Amrita Agro Industries began with a simple belief — that what comes from the earth, in its purest form, is what nourishes best. Our production unit in Shekhupur Droli, Fatehabad carries the spirit of generations of farming families.

We blend time-honored agricultural knowledge with modern manufacturing practices — strengthening the rural economy, creating local employment, and supplying reliable, high-quality agro products to distributors and retailers across the region.

Animal Nutrition

Mesh Cattle Feed

High-quality mesh cattle feed manufactured using premium agricultural by-products. Nutritionally balanced to support healthy livestock.

  • Rich in essential nutrients & protein
  • Supports healthy growth in cattle
  • Improves milk yield in dairy animals
  • Made from quality raw materials
Feed Supplement

Kachi Ghani Binola Khal

Cotton seed cake cold-pressed using the Kachi Ghani process, preserving natural nutrients. A protein-rich supplement widely used across Haryana.

  • High protein content for cattle
  • Natural cold-pressed process
  • Widely used by dairy farmers
  • Available in bulk quantities
Edible Oil

Mustard Edible Oil

Pure mustard edible oil extracted using the traditional Kachi Ghani (cold-press) method. Retains natural aroma, taste, and nutritional benefits.

  • Traditional Kachi Ghani process
  • Rich natural aroma & flavor
  • Free from artificial additives
  • Suitable for cooking & food use
